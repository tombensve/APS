Users and Roles

This administration app lets you create, modify and delete both roles and users. These are the roles and users managed by the aps-simple-user-service-provider.

To create a new user, right-click on "Users" and select "New user".

Then press the "Create" button to save.

To modify a user, just click on it to display it in the main area and then make your changes. Finally click on the "Save" button to save.

To delete a user, right-click on the user and select "Delete user".

Then click the "Delete" button to do the delete.

To create a new role, right-click on "Roles" and select "New role".

Then press the "Create" button to save.

To modify a role, just click on it to display it in the main area and then make your changes. Finally click the "Save" button to save.

To delete a role, right-click on the role and select "Delete role".

Then click the "Delete" button to do the delete.
